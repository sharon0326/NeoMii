About NeoMii & How It Works

NeoMii is a creative-tech startup that transforms your 2D images into fully customizable 3D models using our AI-powered pipeline. No professional tools needed - just upload a selfie or drawing and customize your avatar with our intuitive editor. Want to make your cartoon self slimmer, stronger, taller, or even sci-fi-themed? You can. We also offer premium 3D printing services using advanced resin-based techniques for BJD-quality figurines delivered in just 10 days.

1

Upload & Transform

Upload any 2D image. Our AI, more specifically, rectified flow transformers, will creates a base 3D model.

  • Works with any image type
  • AI-powered conversion
  • Instant preview
2

Customize Your Model

Use our editor to adjust shapes, styles, and features. No 3D experience required. No CAD experiences needed!

  • Intuitive drag-and-drop
  • Body shape adjustments
  • Style themes available
3

Export or Print

Download STL file instantly or order a high-quality 3D print delivered to your door.

  • Instant STL download
  • Premium resin printing
  • 10-day delivery
